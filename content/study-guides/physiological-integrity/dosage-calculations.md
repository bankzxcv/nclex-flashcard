# Dosage Calculations

## Overview
Accurate medication dosage calculation is a critical nursing skill and heavily tested on the NCLEX. Mathematical errors in dosing can result in serious patient harm or death. Mastering various calculation methods and understanding medication math principles is essential for safe practice.

### Insulin Calculations

**Insulin measured in UNITS**, not mg!

**Types of syringes**:
- **U-100 syringe**: Most common (100 units/mL)

**Mixing Insulin** (NPH + Regular):
